Is motorcycle oil different than motor oil?
A motorcycle oil and a car oil have entirely different characteristics. The motorcycle oil will have higher viscosity, slightly higher frictional characteristics and low ash content. The car oil will have lower viscosity levels, low friction, and high ash content in it.
